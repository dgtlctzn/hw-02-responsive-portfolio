About Me

I am currently a Georgia Tech bootcamp student based in Atlanta, GA. I grew up in Atlanta and went to college at the University of Georgia in Athens. Shortly afterward I became very involved with music and started playing in a band. My involvement with music brought me to Brooklyn NY where I joined a band called Savants. My band toured all over the U.S. and even got to record an album in L.A. with our label. I am back in Atlanta now and am getting into coding, learning Python and JavaScript.


Check out some of my coding projects on Github such as a Covid rate tracker and a Twitter bot! I am also active on Instagram
